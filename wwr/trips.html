H2Oardventures - We love rafting!

Full and Half-Day Trips

Our full and half-day trips are a great way to get out on the water and experience the thrill of whitewater rafting. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced rafter, we have a trip that's perfect for you. Our knowledgeable guides will ensure your safety while providing an unforgettable adventure.

Overnight Camping Trips

Overnight camping trips offer a unique opportunity to immerse yourself in nature. Spend your days rafting through exciting rapids and your nights under the stars. Our camping trips include all necessary gear, food, and expert guidance to ensure a safe and enjoyable experience.

Waving From Shore

For the truly adventurous, we offer a unique "Waving From Shore" experience. This option is perfect for those who want to enjoy the beauty of the river without getting wet. Our guides will show you the best spots to watch the action from the shore, providing a fun and relaxing way to experience whitewater rafting.
