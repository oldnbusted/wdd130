California, USA

California is the most populated state in the United States and has the largest economy of any state. It is a very diverse state, with many different kinds of people, cultures, and landscapes, including mountains, beaches, and deserts. The state is a major leader in agriculture, technology through Silicon Valley, and the entertainment industry in Hollywood. California officially joined the United States in 1850 after the Mexican-American War and the Gold Rush brought a large influx of settlers.
